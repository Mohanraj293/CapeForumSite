MEMBERSHIPS

ABOUT CAPE FORUM

     CAPE FORUM – OF BY AND FOR YOU TRUST empowers budding young generation with research and Skill Development ambiance by producing research articles in various disciplines and providing Skill development Trainings and Events. The forum consists of eminent Advisors , Trainers and Supervisors in the various fields of Arts and Science. This forum is organized to provide Non-Profitable service to the Researchers, Teachers and Students and also provides Seminars, Conferences, Workshops, Educational Training, Research Activities, Awards and Publications. The main aim of the forum is to upgrade the quality of research and Empowerment among the Students, Research Scholars & faculty members involved in Research and Education from Arts and Social Science viz., Humanities, Commerce, Management, Education and the allied fields. Its important objective is to disseminate, strengthen and enhance knowledge and Empower with Skill.

MEMBERSHIP ELIGIBILITY AND PROCEDURE

     Membership is open for any Individual or an Institution who may be interested in the objectives of the Research Forum and in Aacademic environment Viz., Faculty Fraternity, Researchers or Students and willing to associate in the advancement of Research. Application to membership can be considered by the Executive Committee to become a member of CAPE FORUM provided that the applicant has a justifiable connection with academic related or a business/ industry related activity in the field of Arts and Social Science. Such a person may apply for the Membership of the forum in the prescribed form accompanied by the prescribed fee. All applications received for membership of the Association shall be placed before the Executive Committee by the Secretary for its consideration. The Executive Committee, without any prejudice of caste, color, creed, religion or gender may consider the application to accept or reject it without assigning any reason. The accepted applications shall be properly filed and the names enrolled with all relevant details in a register called the Register of Members to be maintained by the Secretary of the Forum.

MEMBERSHIP BENEFIT

  • Members will get a Membership certificate with Membership ID.
  • Members can take part in events (Conference, Seminar, Workshop, FDP etc.) organised by the Various Forums of CAPE (Viz., Women’s Forum, Research Forum, Students Forum, Teachers Forum & Skill Development Forum) at concessional rate.
  • Member gets an Opportunity to present and publish paper as Proceedings in the National & International Conferences conducted by the Forum.
  • Members can Organise Academic / Research based Workshops, Conferences, Seminars and FDP, in association with the Forum in their Institution.
  • Members can act as the Resource Person / Honorary post/ Advisory Board members in the Conference, Seminar, Workshop, etc. organised by the Forum/ In Association with Other Institutions.
  • Members can avail Support/ Assistance in Publication of Articles in Peer Reviewed International Journals / ISBN Books.
  • Members shall avail ISBN Book Publication assistance from Our Publishers. (OFBYANDFORYOU PUBLICATION).
  • Members get the opportunity to act as the Editor in Chief / Co- Editor/ Editorial Board Members for the Edited Volume with ISBN published by the Forum.
  • Members get an Opportunity to network with professional peers.
  • Members get Leadership opportunities to develop their academic and professional Career.
  • Members receive Annual Magazine (E- Pdf) at free cost.
  • Members get an Opportunity to receive awards declared by the Forum.
  • Institution / Department which has more than 3 Life Members May Sign MOU/MOA with the CAPE FORUM – YOU TRUST and avail benefits.
