- is a CONSORTIUM OF SCHOOL AND COLLEGE TEACHING FRATERNITY IN INDIA, It consists of eminent Professionals and Academicians from various streams. The forum is organized to provide Non-Profitable service to the Academicians and Researchers, and to assist, Co-organize Seminars, Conferences, Workshops, Educational Training, Guide for Research Activities and Assist Publications. The main aim of the forum is to upgrade the quality in Education and research work among the Students, Research Scholars & faculty fraternity involved in Research from Arts and Social Science viz., Humanities, Commerce, Management, Education and the allied fields. The objective is to disseminate, strengthen and enhance knowledge in research. The desirous of Establishment of a Trust is for

  • Research and Development

  • Skill Development & Training

  • Female health & Awareness

  • Education & Child Development and

  • Women Empowerment

CAPE FORUM Conducts various programs for Academicians, Research scholars, Students and Community to fulfill the objectives of OF BY AND FOR YOU TRUST.
