ABOUT THE FORUM

         The forum is the association of professionals engaged in teaching in Schools and colleges of public and private educational institutions. The aim and objective of the forum is to improve the status of education in India by improving teaching methods. The forum provides opportunity for teachers to enhance their teaching skills by providing Conference, Seminars, Trainings and workshops for teachers. This forum acts as the common platform for teachers to share their ideas to promote quality in education.

OBJECTIVES

  • To develop and promote education by uniting teachers in India.
  • To organize seminars, Conference, panel discussions and training programs for capacity building of teachers.
  • To improve teaching methods, by create a common platform for Discussion for Teachers.
  • To provide a means for the expression of their collective opinion on matters affecting the interests of education and of the teaching profession.
  • To safeguard and improve the conditions of employment of its members and to promote their interests.
  • To regulate the relations between members, their fellow members and between members and their employers.
